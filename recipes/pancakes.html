Pancakes

Description

This pancake recipe is the best you will ever make! It cooks the thickest, fluffiest pancakes you could ever imagine!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter or neutral oil
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and milk until light and foamy, about 3 minutes at high speed (by hand or with mixer) Stir in the butter or oil.
  2. Whisk the dry ingredients together to evenly distribute them.
  3. Gently and quickly mix the dry ingredients into the wet solution. Let the batter rest for at least 15 minutes, so it thickens.
  4. Heat a frying pan over medium heat. Drop 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto the lightly greased griddle.
  5. Cook on one side until bubbles begin to form and break, about 2 minutes; then turn the pancakes and cook the other side until brown, about 1 1/2 to 2 minutes. Turn over only once. Serve immediately.
