Pancakes

Description

This pancake recipe is the best you will ever make! It cooks the thickest, fluffiest pancakes you could ever imagine!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter or neutral oil
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
